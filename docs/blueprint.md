# **App Name**: EcoCart Marketplace

## Core Features:

- Product Browsing: Browse and filter eco-friendly products.
- Product Details: View product details, including materials, certifications, and sustainability impact.
- Shopping Cart: Add products to a shopping cart and proceed to checkout.
- Secure Checkout: Process secure payments via integration with payment gateway.
- Sustainability Report: AI tool to generate a report showing estimated savings due to eco-friendly purchasing, based on entered or automatically sensed purchase history.
- Account Management: Allow users to manage their account information, order history, and saved addresses.
- Admin Backend: Backend interface for managing products, users, and orders.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#00008B) to represent clarity and trustworthiness.
- Background color: White (#FFFFFF) to provide a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Dark red (#8B0000) for highlights and call-to-action buttons to convey urgency and importance.
- Body text: 'Open Sans' (sans-serif) for readability and a modern look.
- Headline text: 'Roboto Slab' (serif) for headlines, giving a bold and contemporary feel.
- Use simple, line-based icons related to e-commerce and sustainability.
- Employ a clean and minimalist design with plenty of whitespace to enhance readability and focus on products.
- Use subtle animations to highlight key interactions, such as adding items to the cart or displaying product details.
